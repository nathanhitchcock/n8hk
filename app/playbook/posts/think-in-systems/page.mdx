Principals are defined by system judgment, not just implementation skill.

## Objective

Develop system-level reasoning that anticipates second-order effects.

## System Thinking Questions

- What breaks if this scales 10x?
- Which interfaces are brittle?
- Where does latency, coupling, or ownership create fragility?
- What are the operational consequences of this design?

## Practice Areas

- Map dependencies and failure paths for key workflows.
- Propose one architecture simplification each quarter.
- Evaluate decisions against maintainability and operability, not just speed.

## Signals of Progress

- Better long-term tradeoffs in design reviews.
- Reduced recurring operational pain.
- Earlier detection of systemic risks.

<Callout title="Guardrails">
